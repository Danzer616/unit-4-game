Crystal Collector!

Game rules:
1. You will given a random number at the beginning of the game.
2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will be adding a specific amount of points to your total score.
3. You win the game by matching the total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.
4. The value of each crystal is hiiden from you until you click on it.
5. When the game restarts, the four crystals will have four new hidden values.
